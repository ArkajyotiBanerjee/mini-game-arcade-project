DON'T "DIE"

A simple two-player game of risk and luck.

How to Play
  • Two players take turns rolling a die
  • Rolling a 1 resets the current turn score to 0 & passes the turn
  • Hold to save points and pass the turn
  • First player to reach the target score of 50 wins
